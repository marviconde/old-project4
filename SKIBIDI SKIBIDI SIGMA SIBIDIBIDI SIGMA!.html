My Top 3 Foods!

Date:

Sisig!

Sisig is a Filipino dish made from pork, typically served sizzling on a hot plate. It's crispy, savory, and often served with a raw egg on top. As you can probably tell, I like spicy foods! So the rest of these are probably spicy, so just bear with me.

Butter Chicken!

Butter Chicken is a rich and creamy Indian dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a luscious tomato-based sauce. It’s mildly spicy and pairs beautifully with naan or rice.

Biryani!

Biryani is a flavorful rice dish loaded with spices, and usually includes meat like chicken or beef. It’s a perfect blend of aromatic spices and tender meat, making it a truly comforting dish.
